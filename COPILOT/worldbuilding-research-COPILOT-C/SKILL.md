# Purpose
Conducts research to support worldbuilding projects, including historical analogs, cultural systems, geography, and mythology references.

# When to use this skill
Use when:
- Researching historical or cultural analogs for a fictional world
- Finding references for geography, mythology, or social systems
- Building research-grounded lore for games or writing

Do NOT use when:
- Pure mechanical game design (use game-design-systems)
- Direct fiction writing (worldbuilding-research supports, not replaces)

# Operating procedure
1. Define research questions tied to worldbuilding goals
2. Identify relevant historical or cultural sources
3. Synthesize research into usable worldbuilding reference notes
4. Flag areas where fictional deviation is intentional
5. Document sources for future reference

# Output defaults
Research notes with sources, worldbuilding implications, and adaptation guidance

# Failure handling
If sources conflict, note both and decide intentionally. Don't blend without awareness.
